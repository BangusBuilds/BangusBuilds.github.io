the new wave

At Bagg, we’ve transcended the pedestrian confines of traditional dining to bring you the epitome of gastronomic purity: shredded cheese, served directly in the bag. This isn’t just food — it’s a statement. A celebration of the raw, unfiltered essence of dairy, hand-selected and expertly shredded to preserve its artisanal integrity. For those who understand that true luxury lies in simplicity, Bagg offers an experience that is as refined as it is revolutionary. Why bother with plates and pretentious presentation when you can indulge in the sublime, unadulterated experience of cheese, still nestled in its bespoke packaging? Bagg isn’t just a restaurant; it’s a movement. A sanctuary for the discerning few who dare to elevate their taste to a higher plane. Join us in redefining dining, one impeccably shredded, bagged morsel at a time.

the Bagg story

In the high-stakes world of culinary innovation, inspiration often strikes in unexpected moments. Our Head Chef, Ben, discovered the essence of true luxury during a late-night work session, finding solace in the simple pleasure of shredded cheese. From that moment, Bagg was born—an unwavering commitment to the belief that the finest dining experiences are those stripped to their purest form. We offer only the highest quality, hand-selected shredded cheese, served directly in the bag, because we understand that true sophistication lies in the art of minimalism.

With Bagg, Chef Ben has shared his vision with London, and soon, the world. We are redefining dining, presenting an experience where simplicity meets unparalleled quality, challenging the conventions of traditional restaurants. Join us in this movement, where every bag embodies our dedication to culinary purity and the elegance of simplicity.
